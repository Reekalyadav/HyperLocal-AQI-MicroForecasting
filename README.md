#### **Project Name: Hyper-Local AQI Micro-Forecasting**



* ###### Problem Statement: Standard AQI apps give a city-wide average (10km resolution), which fails to capture street-level pollution variations caused by traffic bottlenecks and local greenery.

###### 

* ###### Solution: This model predicts AQI at a 500-meter resolution by fusing satellite data ($NO\_2$ levels) with micro-features like traffic density, distance to highways, and local park proximity.



#### **Tech Stack:**

* ###### ML Model: Random Forest Regressor (chosen for its ability to handle non-linear spatial data).

###### 

* ###### Data Handling: Pandas, GeoPandas.

###### 

* ###### Visualization: Folium (Interactive Leaflet Maps).



#### **Key Results:**

* ###### Achieved an R2 Score of 0.75.

###### 

* ###### Successfully visualized pollution hotspots (Red) vs. green zones (Green) on a real-world map of Ghaziabad.
